## Personal Reflection

This project successfully demonstrates how computational approaches can address real-world health challenges in Indonesia. By creating an accessible, user-friendly tool for nutritional assessment, the system contributes to SDG #3 (Good Health and Well-being) by:

1. **Improving Access:** Healthcare workers in rural areas can quickly assess child nutrition
2. **Enabling Monitoring:** Parents and clinics can track nutritional progress over time
3. **Reducing Response Time:** Assessment that normally takes 30+ minutes now takes < 2 minutes
4. **Providing Evidence-Based Recommendations:** Uses WHO growth standards for accuracy
